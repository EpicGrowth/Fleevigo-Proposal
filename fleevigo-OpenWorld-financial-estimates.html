Option A: Fixed-Price Development
Full development delivered for a fixed price with milestone-based payments
What's Included
  • All development as specified in Development Plan
  • Specialists sourced and paid by Epic Growth
  • Project management and coordination
  • Security audits at defined checkpoints
  • Testing and quality assurance
  • Deployment and documentation
  • 30-day post-launch bug fixes (critical issues)
What's Not Included
  • Scope changes beyond defined features
  • Third-party service costs (OpEx)
  • Content creation or data population
  • Ongoing maintenance beyond 30-day warranty

Payment Terms

Fixed-Price Terms

  • Invoices issued at milestone completion
  • Payment due within 14 days of invoice
  • Phase 2 requires signed amendment

Retainer Terms

  • Monthly invoice at month-end
  • Payment due within 14 days
  • Development hours reported weekly
  • 30-day notice for retainer changes

Additional Terms

  • All prices exclude VAT (where applicable)
  • Third-party service costs (OpEx) invoiced separately at cost
  • Currency: EUR
  • Proposal valid for 30 days
